# Source Policy

Use official, primary sources for factual investing content and current accessibility or hosting guidance. Prefer Investor.gov and SEC material for securities basics, FINRA for brokerage and market mechanics, W3C for WCAG, and the host's own documentation for deployment.

Only verify facts used in the current lesson. Avoid time-sensitive limits, rates, tax rules, market statistics, prices, performance examples, or claims that could encourage a particular investment. Write original explanations, examples, activities, and questions. Link directly to every source used and record the verification date in the app.

Content must distinguish education from advice, acknowledge investing risk, avoid promised outcomes, and never recommend a security, fund, brokerage, account, or portfolio.
