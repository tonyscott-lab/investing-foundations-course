# Complete tester brief

## Outcome

A complete, shareable 14-lesson introductory investing course for the creator's mother and brother to test on their phones.

## Learning design

- Adult-beginner, college-100-level structure
- Four to seven short sections per lesson
- Learning outcome, core reading, worked example, key idea, key words, and summary in every section
- Low-stakes knowledge checks within selected sections
- Readable lesson assessment: one large question at a time, immediate explanation, unlimited retakes
- A calm reading cooldown between each lesson and its assessment
- A permanent, searchable Reading Room with ten original seven-minute book guides and four critical-reading labs
- Each guide explains the argument, deeper context, principles, applications, strengths, limitations, reader fit, and how to continue with the full book
- Original course-made concept maps, guide roadmaps, and reading progress rather than copied book graphics
- Retrieval, worked examples, teach-back, and progress visibility instead of casino-style rewards

## Technical design

- Static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- GitHub Pages compatible
- Installable PWA with offline cache
- Device-only progress and no analytics
- Free device speech synthesis for every section
- Static studio narration at one high-value concept
- No runtime API, key, billing, login, database, or paid dependency

## Content boundary

General education only. No individualized financial, legal, or tax advice and no investment recommendation. Core content is based on official SEC/Investor.gov, FINRA, and CFPB educational sources. Reading guides are original commentary and do not reproduce or distribute the source books. No PDFs, affiliate links, or commercial book-sales relationships are included.
